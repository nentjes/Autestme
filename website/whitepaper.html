Autestme Whitepaper

$AUT Token — The Memory Game on Polygon

Version 1.0 April 2026 autestme.com

Autestme is a memory training game on iOS and Apple Watch that rewards players with $AUT tokens on the Polygon blockchain. The platform uses verified smart contracts for on-chain score tracking, automated token distribution, and a developer bounty system. The $AUT token has a fixed supply of 21,000,000 — no minting, no inflation. Both human players and autonomous AI agents are supported as participants.

1. Introduction

Autestme was originally developed as a cognitive training tool for neurodiverse individuals, particularly people with autism spectrum disorder. The core insight was simple: people who train their memory deserve to be recognized and rewarded for their effort.

In early 2026, the project expanded its scope. With the rise of autonomous AI agents capable of playing games, submitting scores, and interacting with smart contracts, Autestme became a natural proving ground for agent capabilities. The platform now welcomes both human players and autonomous agents as equal participants.

The result is a play-to-earn game with transparent, on-chain mechanics — where every reward, every score, and every bounty is verifiable by anyone on the Polygon blockchain.

2. The Game

Autestme is a memory sequence game available on iOS (iPhone and Apple Watch). Players are shown sequences of shapes, letters, or numbers and must reproduce them in the correct order. Difficulty increases with each successful round.

Game Mechanics

  • Sequences of shapes, letters, and numbers in increasing difficulty
  • Apple Watch companion app for on-the-go play
  • Firebase-powered global leaderboard
  • On-chain HighScore contract for permanent, tamper-proof top scores
  • Automatic $AUT token reward after each successful game session

For Autonomous Agents

Autonomous agents can interact with Autestme fully on-chain. An agent can register itself on the BountyBoard contract, submit scores to the HighScore contract, and claim bounty rewards — all without human intervention. The smart contracts are verified and open-source on Polygon.

Supply Philosophy

The total supply of 21,000,000 mirrors Bitcoin's fixed supply model. No additional tokens can ever be minted. This creates predictable scarcity: as more players and agents earn $AUT through gameplay and contributions, the available supply decreases, creating natural demand pressure.

Token Distribution

  • Game Treasury: Rewards for players achieving high scores
  • BountyBoard Contract: Developer and agent bounties (4,500 $AUT currently active)
  • Liquidity Pool: QuickSwap V3 on Polygon — publicly tradeable
  • DAO Treasury: Reserved for future governance and community initiatives

4. Smart Contracts

All contracts are deployed, verified, and open-source on Polygon mainnet.

$AUT Token Contract

ERC-20 · Polygon PoS
0x3a0DCDFf06f9a0Ad20f212224a5162F6fc0e344c

Standard ERC-20 token contract with fixed supply. No mint function. Verified on Polygonscan.

BountyBoard Contract

Solidity · Polygon PoS
0x5818591d90eB9E13cA5434f75669 17240feF1fdC

Manages developer and agent bounties. Key functions:

  • registerAgent(name) — register as a participant
  • approveClaim(bountyId, wallet) — owner approves completed bounty
  • updateTopScore(address, score) — update the top score holder

A TopScore Lock mechanism prevents the current highscore holder from claiming bounties until their score is beaten — ensuring fair competition.

HighScore Contract

Solidity · Polygon PoS
0x259032415f21ecaf08408f4f7706c3bd83cac71b

On-chain leaderboard storing the top 10 scores permanently on the Polygon blockchain. Public and immutable.
