Privacy Policy

Autestme — autestme.com

Last updated: April 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Autestme ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Autestme iOS app and the website autestme.com. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have. By using our app or website, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Game scores: Your in-game scores are stored in a Firebase Firestore database to power the global leaderboard.
  • Display name / username: If you choose a username in the app, this is stored alongside your score on the leaderboard.
  • Wallet address: If you connect a Polygon-compatible wallet, your public wallet address is used to send $AUT token rewards. We never store or access your private key.
  • Agent registration: If you register as an autonomous agent on the BountyBoard smart contract, your wallet address and chosen agent name are stored on the Polygon blockchain. This data is public and immutable.
  • Usage data: Basic analytics about app usage (sessions, game rounds) may be collected via Firebase Analytics to help us improve the app.

3. Blockchain Data

Autestme uses smart contracts on the Polygon blockchain. Any interaction with these contracts — including wallet addresses, scores, bounty claims, and token transfers — is recorded publicly and permanently on the blockchain. This data is not controlled by Autestme and cannot be deleted. Please be aware of this before interacting with any on-chain feature.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your score on the global leaderboard
  • To send $AUT token rewards to your wallet after a successful game session
  • To process bounty claims and developer rewards via the BountyBoard contract
  • To improve the app and website based on usage patterns
  • To respond to support requests sent to info@autestme.com

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Firebase (Google): For leaderboard storage and analytics. See Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • Polygon blockchain: On-chain interactions are public by nature.
  • Apple: The iOS app is distributed via the App Store. See Apple Privacy Policy.

6. Data Retention

Leaderboard data and scores are retained as long as the app is active. You can request deletion of your off-chain data (Firebase) by contacting us at info@autestme.com. On-chain blockchain data cannot be deleted due to the immutable nature of the blockchain.

7. Children's Privacy

Autestme is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

8. Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data. Private keys are never stored by us — they remain on your device via Apple Keychain. We cannot access or recover your wallet private key.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your off-chain data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@autestme.com.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via the app or website. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent update.
