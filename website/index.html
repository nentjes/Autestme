How to Play

1

Choose Your Game

Select from three game modes: Shapes, Letters, or Numbers. Adjust the duration, speed, and number of items to match your skill level.

2

Watch and Remember

Items will appear one by one on screen. Your task is to count how many times each item appears. Stay focused!

3

Enter Your Answers

After the timer ends, enter how many times you saw each item. Get them right and earn $AUT tokens!

Get a Crypto Wallet

To receive $AUT tokens, you need a crypto wallet that supports the Polygon network. Here's how to set one up:

Install the MetaMask app on your phone (iOS or Android) or as a browser extension.
